Information & Details:




Introduction:

Trilingual Program is a newly opened program of the standard curriculum at Srinakharinwirot University Satit Prasarnmit Demonstration School (Secondary). The program will mainly focus on language development including: English, Thai, and Chinese. It also covers some other subjects like finance and othercommunication skills. The program was first introduced in around October 2021 and the first entrance exam was taken online (7th November 2021). There are four main subjects: Math, Science, English, and History. After the online exam applicants are required to submit an introduction video and create a portfolio about themselves. The final result was announced at 26th January 2022, and thus the journey of the first generation of Tri student began.

House System:




Trilingual House System Overview:

Trilingual Program divides students and teachers into 4 houses (according to their colors) to engage in activities together within and between houses, with a system of accumulating House Points based on participation in activities, competitions, and student behavior. The House with the most points at the end of the school year wins the House Cup.


House Prefects:

A selected group of students that will be the role model for other students in the House. There’ll be 6 prefects per each house including 3 Males and 3 Females (2 from each grade). In total there’ll be 24 prefects in the program. The House Prefects are re-selected at the beginning of each school year. Prefects will be tasked to assist teachers in setting up activities and events and most of the time working overtime is required. All House Prefects will be given a certificate during the Appreciation Ceremony.

Seasonal Camp:




Destination: London - United Kingdom | Harbin, Beijing - China | Singapore

Throughout the academic year, there are many overseas exchange programs. However, students who wish to participate must pay an extra fee.
