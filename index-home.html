Fequently Asked Questions:



What is the differences between TRI and SPIP?

Besides the basic subjects under Thailand's Basic Education Core Curriculum B.E. 2551, the Trilingual learning model offers additional subjects in English and Chinese. This is in line with the objective of developing students to be 21st-century global citizens.


Can one apply to the program without any prior knowledge of Chinese?

Yes. Students in the first year of Secondary School (Mathayom 1) in the Trilingual learning model will start learning Chinese characters together. Throughout the three-year period, students will practice communication skills in various situations and learn the necessary grammar to handle those situations.
