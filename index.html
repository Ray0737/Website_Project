Information & General Details

Trilingual Program is a newly opened program of the standard curriculum at Srinakharinwirot University Satit Prasarnmit Demonstration School (Secondary). The program will mainly focus on language development including: English, Thai, and Chinese. It also covers some other subjects like finance and othercommunication skills.

The program was first introduced in around October 2021 and the first entrance exam was taken online (7th November 2021). There are four main subjects: Math, Science, English, and History. After the online exam applicants are required to submit an introduction video and create a portfolio about themselves.

The final result was announced at 26th January 2022, and thus the journey of the first generation of Tri student began.

Studying Details & Curriculum Structure:

  • A | The program is available for students from M.1 to M.3, and those M.3 who meet the grade requirements can choose continue in the Tri major from M.4 to M.6
  • B | After every midterm exam students will receive a student progress report that will explain how well the student performs.
  • C | At the end of every semester. There will be a certificate of appreciation ceremony for students who accomplish or attend in the programâ€™s activities.
  • D |At the end of every academic year students are required to take a CEFR Exam and at the end of M.3 student will have to take a HSK 2 Exam.
